The Best Movies According to Eli

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spider-man

This is my first favorite movie, because Toby Maguire plays an excellent role as Peter Parker and it is something that cannot be missed.

The Amazing Spider-man

This is my second favorite movie, because it has good CGI and even has different elements from the comics.

Spider-man 3

This is my third favorite movie of all time. This has to be one of the best movies ever made as the black Spider-man suit was undeniably cool. It had to have been made with Stan Lee.
